Air Quality across Iowa and Illinois

Air Quality

Air Quality is the measure of pollution in the air
around you. Because of the drastic health effects
poor air quality can cause, it is important to
know the quality of air you're breathing in.
Air quality is measured by assessing a number of
different indicators of pollution. These
indicators are called pollutants, and can be many
different types of chemicals released into the air.


The most common of these pollutants are CO,
NO2, SO2, Ozone, and PM. To get an idea of the
risk to these pollutants in your area and more
information about each, click the buttons
to the left.
